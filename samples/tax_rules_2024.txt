CANADA — FEDERAL TAX (CRA) — TAX YEAR 2024
====================================================================

FEDERAL TAX BRACKETS (Progressive/Marginal):
  $0 to $55,867           → 15%
  $55,867.01 to $111,733  → 20.5%
  $111,733.01 to $154,906 → 26%
  $154,906.01 to $220,000 → 29%

HOW TO CALCULATE: Apply each rate ONLY to the income within that bracket.
Example: $80,000 income
  First $55,867 × 15% = $8,380.05

GENERAL RULES FOR AI CALCULATIONS
====================================================================

1. ALWAYS calculate tax progressively (marginal rates), never flat rate on total income
2. ALWAYS subtract applicable personal amount credits before reporting net tax
3. For Canada: Total tax = Federal + Provincial — show both separately
4. For refund/owed: Compare net tax payable against tax already withheld (from T4/pay stubs)
5. Refund = Tax withheld - Net tax payable (positive = refund, negative = owed)
6. Effective rate = Net tax payable / Gross income × 100
7. If country is Canada, check which province and apply correct provincial rates
8. If filing status is married (USA), use married filing jointly brackets
9. Round all final amounts to 2 decimal places
10. Flag any anomalies: negative income, deductions > income, effective rate > 50%
